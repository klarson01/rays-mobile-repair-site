The Work

Hands-On Local Repair Work

Ray's Mobile Repair is growing and looking for dependable help. We serve Monroe, Green County, and surrounding areas with mobile repair and maintenance for trucks, trailers, equipment, and commercial vehicles.

If you know your way around diesel engines, trailers, electrical issues, brakes, hydraulics, DOT inspections, or general heavy-duty repair, we'd like to hear from you.

At Ray's, the work is hands-on, local, and built around helping customers get back to work. We're looking for people who take pride in honest service, safe work, and repairs done right.

Who Ray Is Looking For

Repair Experience Matters

  • Diesel or heavy-duty repair experience
  • Truck and trailer repair knowledge
  • Electrical and diagnostic ability
  • DOT inspection knowledge, or willingness to learn
  • Welding and fabrication experience a plus
  • Reliable, honest, and self-motivated
  • Able to work independently and safely

Don't have a formal resume? That's okay. Tell us what kind of repair work you've done and the equipment you're comfortable working on.
